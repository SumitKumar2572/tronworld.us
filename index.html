How it works

The investor transfers funds to Wizerpeople, which then invests those funds in companies. These companies generate profits, return them to Wizerpeople, and subsequently, WP distributes the profits back to the investor

Investment in WP

The investor makes a deposit at Wizerpeople, they forward it to the Trusted Escrow account of the startup to provide proper spending control

Funds allocation

Invested funds allocated between company’s operational needs (e.g Purchase of raw materials, digital and physical goods, software, hardware, salaries, rentals)

Profit generation

The startup earns profit using invested funds. Profit statistics are delivered to Wizerpeople by API and could be verified in realtime

Payouts to investors

Investors getting profit accruals according to startup’s profit model. Smooth profit accruals are provided by Wizerpeople Stability fund
